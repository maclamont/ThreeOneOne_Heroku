New York City - Street Conditions

This is a map of the complaints about street conditions in New York City in 2015. As expected, from people who drive in New York, by far the biggest complaint is that of potholes! There is no distinction between boroughs, the blight of the potholes affects everyone.

As we might expect, the potholes appear at different times of the year. Typically, the roads are so badly made that when the temperature starts to warm up and the road surface expands, then the potholes appear.
